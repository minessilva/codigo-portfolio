    Maria Inês Silva
    —
    Born in 1994 on a small island, called São Miguel, then moved to Lisbon where she studied Design and currently works at TVM Designers. Looking for a new studio to adventure and have a design opportunity abroad.
    All artists, especially designers are made by their references that empower and develop their analyses of everyday situations. Throughout my work I try to show how my interests take part on the creative process. Views of the world and their cultural shapes that’s what really matters to the design field. From visual arts to music, this small cultural pieces create a bigger picture.
